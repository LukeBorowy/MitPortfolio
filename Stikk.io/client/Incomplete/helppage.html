How to play Stikk.io

In Stikk.io, you play as a stick person as you collect money, battle others, and try to make it to the top of the leaderboard!

Controls

Mouse and Keyboard

Touch

Move your mouse in the direction you want to move. Left click and hold to attack. Right click and hold to boost. Or, you can hold space to boost. Click on the shop to open up the categories of items. Click on the categories to see all items of that type. Hover over an item to see its stats. Click on it if you want to buy it. If you have a ranged weapon, press 'r' to manually reload your ammo.

Blah Blah

Earning Money

Defeat players to take their money and upgrade your equipment. Defeating a player will give you half of their total value. If there are no players, destroy shapes to gain money. The more sides a shape has, the more money it gives you. However, more valuable shapes also have more health.
